Meet Your Cabo San Lucas VIP Hosts

Cynthia Ochoa – CEO & VIP Host

As Founder and CEO of CABO BASH, lifestyle management services and hospitality became my major strengths acquired throughout 10 years of experience in marketing and promoting the nightlife in Cabo and hospitality industries, leading me to become one of the most respected Personal VIP Hosts in Cabo and in Mexico catering to a select and demanding high profile clientele that is ready to continue experiencing the lavish lifestyle of the rich and famous when they come to Cabo San Lucas. In other words my VIP services are aimed towards individuals who savor the finer things in life and demand nothing less than the best.

Just like an Amex Black Card on steroids, my Hosting Services will get what you need and more.

Emilia Sadowska – VIP Host

Being the Polish traveler that I am, Cabo became my new home after cancelling my return flight to rainy London during my two week vacation in this paradise of freedom and sunny weather, where I find it an amazing place to be. And believe me, I have crossed almost all of Europe to have a point of comparison!

My hosting experience in this business includes working at the best nightclubs in London like Whiskey Mist or DSTRKT, Boujis and Tonteria where I catered to celebrities Nicole Scherzinger, Drake, Kid Ink, Lady Gaga, Leo DiCaprio, Robin Thicke and professional poker player Phil Ivey, while in Cabo I’ve had the opportunity of hosting for Enrique Iglesias, Cindy Crawford and Adam Levine to name a few. So be sure that if you want to experience Cabo the VIP way, you are in good hands as I will provide the best service to suit your needs!

So no more speaking! Let’s get ready to experience paradise!

Jay Vasquez – VIP Host

Well traveled, active, outgoing, social, nightlife extraordinaire and  the ultimate wing man are only a few of the things which describe me. Been in too many different countries to know there is nothing compared to the lifestyle in Cabo, its cool vibe and its hippest spots.

I was also a tourist when I fist visited this paradise 7 years ago, so I know what you have in mind and how to accomplish it the Rock Star way. So if you wanna break loose with style and have the ultimate experience as a bachelorette and bachelor in Los Cabos, you’ve found your best ally.

Let’s roll and take your trip to the next level!

Emilio de Icaza – VIP Host

Originally from Mexico City and raised in America’s finest city San Diego, California where I adapted to the Southern California lifestyle for close to 29 years.

Be sure to chose my services to experience the ultimate red carpet treatment you deserve.

Juan & Only – VIP Host

My main focus is to offer you Cabo on a silver plate and guarantying the ultimate experience. From beach clubs and nightclubs to the best restaurants and activities.

My clients include many celebrities and fortune 500 CEO’s so discretion and protocol are my strong suit. Doing it all but with style.

Karla Gonzalez – VIP Host

I’m an extrovert social butterfly who loves making sure everyone around me is having a great time.
